================================================================================
OPTIMIZATIONS APPLIED
================================================================================

1. Fused dequant+dot          Eliminated temporary buffers
2. Multi-threading            4 cores for matmul operations
3. NEON SIMD                  ARM vectorization (ready for testing)
4. Flash attention            Reduced memory footprint (O(n) vs O(n²))
5. Parallel vocab projection  256K vocabulary across 4 threads (1.84x)
6. Parallel beam processing   Process 4 beams simultaneously (1.34x)
7. Buffer reuse               Eliminated per-step allocations

Total Speedup: 4.19x from baseline, 1.46x vs CTranslate2
